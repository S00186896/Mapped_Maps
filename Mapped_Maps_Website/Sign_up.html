China Border Disputes

China has some of the most border disputes in the world. Due to the centralised communist government in China they censor all information comining into the country, this is called the great firewall of China. China claims the full country of Taiwan, as well as claiming regions in India, Bhutan and Tajikistan.



Russia Border Disputes

Russia has many border disputes due to the dissoultion of USSR in 1991. The media is under strict control in Russia, the Russian government uses these border disputes for propaganda. The border disputes can be found on the Georgian border and the Ukrainian border. In 2014 Russia invaded the disputed region of Crimea.



Isreali Border Disputes

Isreal is a country that isnt recognised in most Arab countries. This area is one of the most disputed regions in the world. The entire country is also claimed by Palestine too. The areas that are heavily disputed are the Gaza strip and the West Bank.
